HIMX : In-Depth
Notes
Himax is a fabless IC chip manufacturer.
A Himax micro-display is in the coming Google Glass system.
The Oculus Rift gaming headset also has a Himax chip in it, but I don't know if this chip amounts to any significant revenue potential for Himax.

Risks
Innolux accounts for 34.2% of Himax's revenue.
Innolux is also a Himax shareholder of about 15% outstanding shares as of March 2103.

Himax does not manufacture its own wafers.
They do not do their own assembly or testing.
