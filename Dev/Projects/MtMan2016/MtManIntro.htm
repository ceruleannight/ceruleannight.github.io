Mountain Man is a game concept I have had for probably over 15 years. It is inspired somewhat from The Oregon Trail. This is actually the third try at writing the game. On the first attempt I was in Venezuela over Christmas in 2003 trying to program on a foreign keyboard with many keys in weird places. I didn't get very far. Then in 2014 I took another attempt. By this point with three kids and many other responsibilities I found that I was biting off more than I wanted to get into at that point and eventually I abandoned the project. Then in the summer of 2016 while needing a break from some other stuff I decided to scale back my ambitions for the game and frame up something much simpler but complete-able for me. This is the result. Enjoy. -- Pat Colllins
